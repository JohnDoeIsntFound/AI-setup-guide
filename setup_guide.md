# AI Setup Guide

<br>

(Note: this guide is for computer 💻)

<br>

### I. Access Claude

Option 1: via the [website](https://claude.ai/)

Option 2: via the [desktop app](https://claude.com/download)

<br>

### II. Configure Claude

##### 1. Click on the profile icon

![Screenshot, Chat Screen, Highlighted Profile Icon](./img/chat_screen_profile_icon.png)

<br>

##### 2. Click on the settings button

![Screenshot, Chat Screen, Highlighted Settings Button](./img/chat_screen_settings_button.png)

##### 3. Find the instructions section

![Screenshot, Settings Screen, Highlighted Instructions Section](./img/settings_screen_instructions_section.png)

##### 4. Choose your instructions

Choose & Copy your instructions [here](./instructions_selection_computer_redirection.md)

then paste it into the instructions section

![Screenshot, Settings Screen, Highlighted Instructions Section, Filled](./img/settings_screen_instructions_section_filled.png)

Close the settings after this

<br>

##### 5. Choose the AI's thinking mode

Click on the thinking mode option

![Screenshot, Settings Screen, Highlighted Instructions Section, Filled](./img/chat_screen_model_selection.png)

Hover over the effort option

![Screenshot, Chat Screen, Effort Option](./img/chat_screen_effort_option.png)

<br>
    
Choose whether the AI thinks (Personally I like to keep thinking on so that it could read nuances better and get more accurate information)

And choose a thinking level (I use medium most of the time and high for special occasions (e.g. sharing my writings))

![Screenshot, Chat Screen, Effort Panel](./img/chat_screen_effort_panel.png)

<br>
    
Et Voilà! It's all complete now! Enjoy your interactions with Claudette/Claudio

![Screenshot, Chat Screen, The End, Good Job, Huggies! Muah](./img/chat_screen_end.png)
